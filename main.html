Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, bookings can be cancelled according to Aurora Travels cancellation policy via your personal dashboard. Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days.

Is payment secure?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We do not store your full card details on our servers.

Do I need travel insurance?

While not mandatory for all locations, we highly recommend travel insurance to cover unforeseen medical costs or trip interruptions.

How do I get my travel documents?

All tickets, vouchers, and itinerary documents are sent directly to your registered email address 48 hours after booking confirmation.

Are customized tour packages available?

Yes! You can contact our 24/7 support team to tailor any of our popular destinations to your specific preferences and budget.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Our premium support team monitors your flight status in real-time. If there is a delay, we automatically notify your hotel and transport transfers.
